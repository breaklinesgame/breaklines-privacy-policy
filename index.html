Privacy Policy

Break Lines

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Break Lines ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Summary: Break Lines does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require accounts, logins, or any form of registration. The app uses third-party services (advertising and analytics) that may collect limited, anonymized data as described below.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. The app does not require user registration or login.

Locally Stored Data: The app stores the following data locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Your highest game score
  • Total number of games played
  • Sound preference setting
  • Tutorial completion status

2. Third-Party Services

Break Lines uses the following third-party services that may collect certain data:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID, subject to your App Tracking Transparency consent)
  • IP address (for approximate location at country or city level)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from partner apps.

Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the app. This helps us improve the game experience. Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • App usage events (e.g., game started, game ended, score achieved)
  • Device information (model, operating system version, screen size)
  • App version and language settings
  • Anonymized usage statistics

Firebase Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Information.

3. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the app respects Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If prompted, you may choose to allow or deny tracking. If you deny tracking, only non-personalized ads will be served, and no device advertising identifier will be shared with third parties.

4. Push Notifications

The app may request your permission to send local push notifications (e.g., reminders to play). These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve any remote server or data transmission. You can disable notifications at any time through your device's Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Break Lines does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal data to function. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information through a third-party service, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent any user data. The only data shared with third parties is the limited, anonymized information collected by Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics as described in Section 2, solely for the purposes of serving advertisements and improving the app experience.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on any server, there is minimal risk to your personal information. All game data (scores, settings) is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features.

8. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear the app's data. Data collected by third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics) is retained according to their respective data retention policies.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising by denying App Tracking Transparency permissions or adjusting your device's ad settings
  • Disable push notifications through your device's Settings
  • Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about data collected by third-party services by contacting Google directly

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
